Cyber Security Basics

Protect Yourself in the Digital World

Introduction

Cyber security is the practice of protecting computers, mobile devices, networks, and online accounts from cyber attacks. As more people use the internet for banking, shopping, education, and communication, understanding basic cyber security has become essential.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing Emails
  • Malware
  • Ransomware
  • Identity Theft
  • Password Attacks

Use Strong Passwords

Always create strong and unique passwords for each online account. Avoid using simple words or personal information that can be guessed easily.

Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your operating system, browser, and apps. Updates often include important security patches that protect against newly discovered vulnerabilities.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of protection to your online accounts. Even if someone knows your password, they cannot access your account without the second verification step.

Be Careful with Emails and Links

Never click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown senders. Cybercriminals often use fake emails to steal passwords and personal information.

Benefits of Cyber Security

  • Protects personal information
  • Reduces the risk of online fraud
  • Keeps devices safe from malware
  • Protects financial accounts
  • Improves online privacy
